Welcome to our Products Management System!

Here You can carry out operations stated below:

            To start new GUI - /home
        
            To see all products - /product/all
            To find product by its name - /product/name
            To find product by its id - /product/id

            To see all manufactures - /manufacture/all
            To find manufacture by its name - /manufacture/name
            To find manufacture by its id - /manufacture/id

If You entered into our site as admin You can carry out such additional operations:

            To update products - /product/update
            To delete product by id - /product/delete

            To update manufactures - /manufacture/update
            To delete manufacture - /manufacture/delete

            To see all roles - /role/all
            To find role by its name - /role/name
            To find role by its id - /role/id
            To delete role by id - /role/delete

            To see all users - /user/all
            To find user by its name - /user/name
            To find user by its id - /user/id
            To update user - /user/update
            To delete user by id - /user/delete

For logout

            To logout  - /logout
